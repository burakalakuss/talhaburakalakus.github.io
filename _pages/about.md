I am a faculty member in the Department of Software Engineering at Kırklareli University. My research interests include artificial intelligence, bioinformatics, genomic signal processing and software engineering.

My recent work focuses on applying deep learning and tabular models to complex prediction problems, including software defect prediction and chemical property analysis. I am particularly interested in bridging theoretical AI models with practical, real-world applications.

In addition to research, I actively teach courses on mobile application development, flutter and modern software engineering practices. I also contribute to institutional AI strategies as an AI Coordinator.

This website presents my academic work, publications, teaching activities, and ongoing projects.
