## Abstract

This study presents a deep learning-based approach for detecting smishing attacks using SMS text data. After applying NLP-based preprocessing techniques, a hybrid CNN-GRU model is employed to classify messages.

## Keywords

Smishing, Deep Learning, CNN-GRU, Cybersecurity
